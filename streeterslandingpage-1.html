"Started from the bottom, now we're here,
got that drip on me like it's engineered."
— Drake, Started from the Bottom

✍ Annotation

NOW WE'RE HERE

Drake uses this line to mark his rise from poverty in Scarborough, Toronto to global superstardom. "Here" represents success, recognition, and luxury — the entire arc of his come-up compressed into three words. The phrase has since become a cultural shorthand for any major glow-up.
